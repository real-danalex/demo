How Can We Help You?

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products, ordering, delivery, and more.

Products

We offer a wide variety of freshly baked bread including White Bread, Wheat Bread, Multigrain Bread, Sweet Bread, and special seasonal varieties. All our products are baked fresh daily using quality ingredients.

Yes! All our bread products are made without artificial preservatives. We use only natural ingredients to ensure freshness and quality. This is why we recommend consuming our bread within 3 days of purchase or freezing for longer storage.

Currently, our standard products contain gluten. However, we are working on introducing gluten-free and vegan options. Please check back or subscribe to our newsletter for updates on new product launches.

Our bread stays fresh for 3-4 days when stored in a cool, dry place. For longer storage, you can freeze our bread for up to 1 month. Simply thaw at room temperature or toast directly from frozen.

Ordering

Ordering is easy! Simply browse our products, select the items you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or login to complete your order. You can also call us directly at +234 801 234 5678 to place an order.

Payment

We accept multiple payment methods including Bank Transfer, Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), and mobile payments. All card transactions are secure and encrypted.

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your financial information. We never store your complete card details on our servers.

For online payments, your card/account will be charged immediately upon order confirmation. For bank transfers, please make the payment within 24 hours and send proof to info@countryfresh.ng. Cash on delivery is collected at the time of delivery.

Currently, we require full payment at the time of order. However, we offer special pricing for wholesale and regular customers. Contact us to discuss bulk purchase options and payment terms.
